About

With a permanent collection of over 10,000 objects representing a wide range of cultures and periods of art history, the Tweed Museum of Art, located on the campus of the University of Minnesota Duluth, is a major cultural and education resource for the Upper Midwest.

Dates

T 10am-7pm, W-T-Sat 10am-3:30pm. Closed Mondays, Fridays, Sunday, and major holidays.

Rates

Free admission. No admission fee is charged but donations are welcome and suggested at the rate of $5/family, $2/individuals, $1/seniors, children and students free.

Green Practices

  • Energy efficiency/conservation
  • Waste reduction/reuse/recycling

Attraction Attributes

  • Can accommodate groups of 45 or more

Meetings & Conferences

  • Exhibit space (sq ft): 15,000
  • On-site catering required
